Skooma was made and perfected by the Dunmer people, by way of mixing moon-sugar and nightshade. Since then, Skooma has been used across the continent. Skooma users will notice an increase in strength, speed, and stamina. Some side effects of using Skooma are bouts of euphoria, protracted lethargy, loss of voice, minor fits, and in rare cases death.

Skooma is distilled from moon-sugar, which is sacred to we who worship the moon and stars.

Moon-sugar

Moon-sugar is native to Tenmar Forest in southern Elsweyr. The holiest of substances to the Khajit, moon-sugar is a daily part of their lives. Moon-sugar can potentially be dangerous and addictive if not used properly.

A white powder, very sweet, used as spice for food. Some of us believe it gives true insight into ja-Kha'jay, where all fates are bound. Others use it to brew Skooma.

Nightshade

Nightshade is a poisonous plant that can be found around Cyrodiil and Skyrim. It can be used to create poison and coat weapons with poisonous liquid. Skooma uses the root of the Nightshade and can also use the berries produced from the plant to give a more fragrant smell.

The root of the nightshade plant contains most of the essence needed to make Skooma.
